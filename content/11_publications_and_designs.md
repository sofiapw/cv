### 2016 - Inohana Children's Hospital Green Roof
- **Designers**: Sofia M. Penabaz-Wiley, Amonkar Deepika, Jorge Izquierdo, Poh Qiying, Shi Yang
- **Issuer**: National University of Singapore and Chiba University Joint Collaborative Design Workshop: Children's Hospital Design
- **Date**: February 26, 2016
- **Title**: "Landscape as Connectivity"
- **Main Contributions**: Ethnobotanical aspects for five senses, universal design interactive garden play, aquaculture, labyrinth, cupola, ecological support systems. Developed most of the wildlife support, the disabled children's universal design playground, and the aquaponics unit. Used recycled water from the AC units to feed into the space for wildlife. Contributed plant selection.

### 2014 - CCDC 2014 Cross-Cultural Design Collaboration (Iro Park)
- **Designers**: Juan C. Gálvez, Lin Bai, Sofia M. Penabaz-Wiley
- **Issuer**: Chiba University Cross-Cultural Design Collaboration (CCDC)
- **Date**: September 2014
- **Title**: Group 3, "Iro Park"
- **Main Contributions**: Water element, herb garden for children's play, cupola, labyrinth, shape, and location inside the park, a half-moon of cherry trees nearby, and UD benches throughout the park. Created an area in the park for children to play in water, art created by a local artist, a shade space for mothers, and native and herbal plants for pollinators as well as for children to learn. Has signature labyrinth and colorful recycled glass mosaic shade structure.

### 2019 - Recycling Game and Ethnobotanical System
- **Designer**: Self (individual project)
- **Issuer**: NPO China PI Cultural Exchange, Community Development Projects (CDP), Shanghai, China
- **Date**: June 2019
- **Overview**: Developed a tiered ethnobotanical system and recycling game to make recycling fun for children based on the needs of the community to recycle trash and their lack of funds. Utilizing recycled materials and a recycling station, created a place to play and grow ethnobotanical plants.

### 2019 - Guang Community Center Green Veranda
- **Designer**: Self (individual project)
- **Issuer**: NPO China PI Cultural Exchange, CDP, Shanghai, China
- **Date**: July 2019
- **Overview**: Designed a simple aquaponics unit for the community center where people could gather food as well as enjoy the live fish.

### 2020 - Green Kamiyama Maker Space Aquaponics System
- **Designer**: Self
- **Issuer**: Kamiyama Village, Tokushima, 2020
- **Overview**: Designed aquaponics system for community revitalization using rainwater capture, native ethnobotanical plants and endangered fisheries, and agricultural plants in the indoor system. Presentation of design operation aired online for Tokushima and Osaka residents and stakeholders.
